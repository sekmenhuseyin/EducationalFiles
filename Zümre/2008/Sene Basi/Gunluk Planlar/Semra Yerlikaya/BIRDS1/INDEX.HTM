The Amazing World of Birds

Welcome to the beautiful, the wonderful, the truly amazing world of birds. Birds are really beautiful creatures, they are also fascinating and all around us, roosting and nesting on our buildings and feeding in our gardens and refuse dumps. They are easy to find and fun to observe. There are about 9.703 species of birds divided up into 23 orders, 142 families.
