THE BIGGEST
The largest bird is the ostrich. It can grow up to 2.7 m. tall.

 

THE SMALLEST

The smallest bird is the bee hummingbird which is 6.2 cm. long weighing only 1.6 g.

THE BEST NIGHT VISION
Owls have the best night vision; these birds hunt at night.

 

THE BEST HOVERER
The best hoverers are hummingbirds, terns , gulls, and kestrels (sparrow hawks).

THE FASTEST IN THE SKY
   The fastest flying birds include these:
   The peregrine falcon, clocked at 145 kph.
   The spine-tailed swift, about 145-160 kph.
   Champion racing pigeons fly up to 85 kph.
