MISSION STATEMENT

Mehmet Akif college and its ideal; making the albanian youth conscious about national, ethical, moral\ human cultural values and making them give importance to family, fatherland so that one day they will be the people who will exalt these values.

Giving the possibility of a good education to our children who are our future guarantee is the aim of everyone of us.

We are giving education in Turkish Colleges thinking that "The best investment is the one made for people." Because we know that only a person who posseses a good physical , mental , spiritual , moral condition, who is moderate in feelings, who has a personality and a stable character, who is able to think objectively and scientifically , who shows respect to other people`s rights, who gives importance to other people`s ideas and initiatives, who knows his responsibilities, is able to manage and construct a bright future. Our primary goal in our educational organizations is to increase the quality of education and to use everything to achive this. The successes that have been achived by our students make us proud of them.

THE CONCEPT OF SCHOOL’S DISCIPLINE
 

The concept of school’s discipline must be understood in the right way because it is a subjective concept that varies from one person to another and some people consider it as being smart in appearance.
-The discipline is of great importance in a school because it strengthens the rules and improves th behaviours of the students.
-The most important aim of the discipline in M. Akif college is to help the students to make the right decisions and make them behave in right place in right way.
The discipline in our school is not something that should be criticized because it prevents the students from their rights but it should be admired because it helps in the education fields of students
POINT OF VIEW TO THE PROCESS OF THE EDUCATION IN OUR SCHOOL
The aim of our school is the preparation of students in a way that in the future they can live without any difficulties and wherever they go they can be leader. We do not mean for politic leaders. Our aim is to prepare good youngs in the society.That’s why we give importance to justice and behaviours of students.
-Our school is working to prepare good people in the way that students can decide on their ownhow to act to reach the success, how to get on well with others wherever they go in the future.
-The graduated students of this school should be a good example to the others.We prepare students that can afford rivalry in the society we live.But we should understand in a right way the concept of rivalry and the desire of being successful. The most important thing is being a person with high values than a good student.We want “the good man” “the man who thinks”.
