Message From The Director

Because it affects university life and as a result a good career, high school life is very important. High school students are ready to get everything they are taught. Students who get good education in high school may get a place at a good university and as a result have a good job in their future. We are not a newly-established school. We have been teaching kids from kindergarten to high school students for 10 years and we have already won Albanian people’s trust. So far 850 students have already graduated and are still studying in universities of 21 countries. Our board of teachers and administration are making every endeavor to give the best education to your children who you have entrusted us and to prepare them for life.

- Students get the right to enroll in our school after a competition done among more than 1000 8th class pupils allover Albania.

In the 9th class students learn 2 languages, English and Turkish, and they also take science lessons, like maths, physics, chemistry and biology. In the upper classes science subjects are taught in English, while social subjects are taught in Albanian. A student who graduates from our school can understand and speak English and Turkish very fluently and can also communicate in German, which is taught in the 11 and 12 classes. To be in touch with this age’s latest technology, computer knowledge is taught the best way possible.

- Besides education we try to offer accommodation to students who come from other regions outside Tirana.

- Being an institution that Albania’s distinguished people choose, with our qualified board and contemporary education methods we will try to always do our best for Albania.

- We believe that by visiting our school you will be able to see with your own eyes what you can’t by just visiting our website.
