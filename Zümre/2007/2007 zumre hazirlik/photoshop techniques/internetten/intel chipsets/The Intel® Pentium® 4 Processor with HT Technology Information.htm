Products   Hyper-Threading Technology  

Did you know a PC based on the Intel® Pentium® 4 Processor with HT Technology can handle more things at once? In fact, Hyper-Threading Technology (or HT Technology for short) can provide software performance benefits of up to 25% in multitasking situations, delivering advanced performance for both home and business PC users.¹

Today, your business users need to do more in less time. And so do you. Find out how Hyper-Threading Technology can help you run IT and business applications simultaneously, seamlessly.

HT technology enhances the PC's ability to run multiple applications at the same time - like playing a game while converting your favorite music files or opening a large presentation while virus scan runs in the background. You don't have to wait for one task to finish before starting the next one, so your computer is more effective, and you are too.
