PhotoShop - Fire
(back to Bluesfear)

Here you will be taught how to make realistic fire.

 

1)Open a new file, around 400 x 300 pixels. with a white background.

2) Now set your forground color to white and background color to black.

 

3)  Go to FILTER > RENDER > CLOUDS.

4) Go to FILTER > RENDER > LIGHTING EFFECTS and use these settings

5) Make a Copy of this layer.

6) With the copy go to FILTER > BRUSH STROKES > ACCENTED EDGES. Use these settings

6) Now on the copy layer. Select the ERASE TOOL and brush size 200. Then delete the
brightest spot of the fire.
